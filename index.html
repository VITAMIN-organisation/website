Core Mechanisms

Modular & compositional design supporting multiple models and logics without touching the core engine.

Two modes: Expert (upload & verify) and Non‑Expert (guided creation).

Interactive verification with immediate feedback in the browser.

Features

Experts

Upload a model as text, choose a logic, and run verification on advanced specifications.

Non‑Experts

Build models step‑by‑step with guided inputs. Define system characteristics without prior knowledge of formal logics.

Developers

Extend VITAMIN with new models, logics, and verification methods thanks to its modular architecture.
